Zebulun peniel was founded in the year 2010 on the principles of love and compassion after realizing a great demand and need for counseling in our community, with a lot of vice that come along with our rapid change of information and exposure at an early stage of life, Zebulun stepped in and offered a helping process that uses the safety arranged method by a special kind of relationship to help individuals to get access to greater part of their personal resources as a means of responding to the challenges of their lives we use specific skills and techniques, in that relationship we help people become more competent more contented and more creative, we don’t primarily deal with mentally ill but with normal individuals facing all the difficulties involved in domestic, work oriented and social life.

We are against gender based violence hence we help our clients who have become victims of gender violence by placing them into a safe house where empowering and counseling takes place as well as healing.Social welfare services are given to abandoned babies in our foster homes and adoption programes with the help of the government Zebulun is pro-life and the rights of the unborn are valued as much as the mother’s too

WHY CHOOSE US

We are available all year round to people of all walks of life via self-referral and to other members via a referral from any other relevant place or institution,Our Psychological service offers advice, guidance and self-help resources for you to develop the skills to help you to manage your own wellbeing. We find that many individuals are looking for rapid access to resources to feel better in the short-term. Counseling sessions may be appropriate when you are ready to commit time to develop insight into longer-term difficulties or recognized patterns of thoughts and feelings that you would like to change.

It is the role of our Psychological Wellbeing Practitioner (PWP) to help you identify the resources most appropriate to your need, and to keep in touch to monitor your progress and wellbeing.When we receive your Self-Referral Questionnaire you will receive an email offering a range of appropriate interventions. This will include an invitation to a Drop-in session at which you can view resources and talk to our PWP. You can also arrange an individual session with the PWP to discuss your needs in more detail. We cater for your most intimate matters too if need be.You may wish to access some private support. We can provide information on how to find a private counselor or psychotherapist.

Accreditation

We are proudly accredited by the Kenya Counseling Psychological Association of Counseling Services (KCPA), and adhere to the high standards for CODE OF CONDUCT and counseling services.
